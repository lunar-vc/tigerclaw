## Risks & Open Questions

1. **THE-2180 is crowded.** Three well-funded startups (nEye, iPronics, Lumentum incumbent) are already shipping or sampling MEMS/silicon photonic OCS. The window for a new entrant is narrow unless they have a fundamentally different approach (e.g., piezo-electric vs MEMS, or integrated with CXL memory fabric).

2. **THE-1882 timing.** The neuro-symbolic agent space is moving fast. Multiple approaches are converging (OpenSymbolicAI typed primitives, AgenticDomiKnowS constraints, AutoGen conversations). The winner will likely be whoever gets developer adoption first, not who has the best paper.

3. **THE-2171 academic vs industry gap.** Both CXL memory tiering candidates (Zhao, Song) have strong academic signals but unclear entrepreneurial intent. Meta and SK hynix are heavily investing in this space internally. A startup would need a differentiated approach (e.g., application-level checkpoint optimization rather than kernel-level tiering).
